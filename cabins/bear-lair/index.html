Bear Lair

Previous Next

Enjoying many stays in many cabins during the past several years, we decided to try our hand at building our own. We had the perfect spot directly on a 29 million gallon lake surrounded by over 25 acres of spectcular natural scenery. The cabin was built to feature the best of all of the cabins which we had visited. In addition, we strove to add aspects which would make a stay in Bear Lair something unique and something to remember. According to visitors' comments, that has been achieved. Being located in the forest area, every effort has been made to exhibit just how beautiful wood can be. From the unusual ceiling to the hardwood floors and the white pine walls, the owner crafted cedar chest and the counter and bar, it is difficult to think that another suck cabin exists. Taking advantage of the 22 foot porch which overhangs the lake, it is also difficult to imagine a more relaxing place. From the porch, one can fish, feed the fish [feed provided], or just sit and watch for the wildlife which regularly visits the lake. If you are lucky, you just might see a deer bringing her new fawn down for a drink. With the hiking trails leading to the caves and natural arch and the furnished watercraft, it is difficult to think that a person could ever be bored while visiting Bear Lair. We promise you will want to return!
