About Cabins Direct

The Red River Gorge and Natural Bridge area is one of the most beautiful and unique places in the world. However, the communities within the area are suffering. Stricken by poverty, tourism shows promise to be a saving grace. In order for it to become one, though, it is essential that the money brought into the area stays in the area.

For many people living in this area who have built a cabin, there seems to be only two options. They can either get a website for their cabin and hope people find it buried under all of the other cabin websites, or they can sign a contract with a booking agency, relinquishing a lot of control over their own cabin while also being charged a commission of 30, 40, or even 50% of every booking.

My family has experienced this first hand. When my parents opened their first cabin, they chose to go with a booking agency. While they are grateful for the start, the high commission along with the loss of control made it apparent that this was not a long term solution. The booking agencies are good solutions for people who have little invested in their property, but they are not good solutions for people who want to maintain control of their cabins.

This website aims to be a place where prospective renters can browse cabins owned and operated by people living in the area. As of now, this website is completely ad supported, and no commission will ever be charged to the owners. By booking through the websites linked on this, you maximize the amount of money for the owners, giving them more money to help the local economy and to provide a better cabin rental and experience for you.
